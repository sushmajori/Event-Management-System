About Cultural Fest 2019


Singing

In Singing event you can choose any song to sing. You can participate as an individual contestent or you can sing duet or you can make a group. No restrictions for number of memebrs in a group. You have to give two auditions before the event. If you are qualified both the auditions then only you can perform on the Cultural Fest 2019.


Dancing

In Dancing event you can choose any style of dancing. You can participate as an individual contestent or you can make a pair or you can make a group. No restrictions for number of memebrs in a group. You have to give two auditions before the event. If you are qualified both the auditions then only you can perform on the Cultural Fest 2019.


Drama

In Drama event you can choose any topic or script. You can participate as an individual contestent or you can make a group. No restrictions for number of memebrs in a group. You have to give two auditions before the event. If you are qualified both the auditions then only you can perform on the Cultural Fest 2019.
